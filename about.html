About NSRIT

Nadimpalli Satyanarayana Raju Institute of Technology (NSRIT) is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical education. Established with the aim of fostering excellence in engineering and technology, NSRIT has quickly become a leading name in the field of higher education.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience through a comprehensive curriculum, innovative teaching methods, and industry collaborations, empowering students to become leaders in their fields.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in technical education, research, and innovation.

Why Choose NSRIT?

  • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Modern classrooms, advanced labs, and well-stocked libraries.
  • Experienced Faculty: A team of highly qualified professors and industry experts.
  • Industry Partnerships: Strong ties with leading companies for internships and placements.
  • Holistic Development: Emphasis on both academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Research and Innovation: Dedicated research centers and funded projects.

Campus Life

NSRIT offers a vibrant campus life with a wide range of student organizations, cultural events, and technical clubs. The institute encourages students to participate in national and international competitions, fostering a competitive and creative spirit.

Accreditations and Affiliations

NSRIT is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU). The college is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Core Values

  • Integrity and Ethics
  • Excellence in Education
  • Social Responsibility
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Diversity and Inclusion
